The Reality Today

What's Not Working Today

These aren't small inconveniences — they are systemic failures that affect every resident every day.

Bad Roads & Potholes
Roads are patched poorly each monsoon. No contractor accountability. No long-term fix. The same potholes return every year.
No Response to Complaints
Complaints disappear after being filed. No tracking, no SLA, no accountability. Residents are left waiting for weeks or months.
Corruption & No Transparency
Ward budgets are invisible. Contractor deals happen behind closed doors. Citizens have no way to audit how their money is spent.
Waste & Sanitation Issues
Missed garbage pickups, open dumps, no segregation system. Poor sanitation is a daily health risk for thousands of families.
The Roadmap

What Will Change

Eight concrete pillars. Each one is a trackable commitment — not a promise, a system.

Pothole-Free Roads
Geo-tagged potholes, contractor SLAs, and real-time repair tracking so no pothole lasts more than 7 days.
Corruption-Free Governance
All contractor bids, tenders, and fund usage published publicly. Zero secret dealings.
Doorstep Services
Government certificates, utilities, complaints — accessible from your phone without visiting any office.
Water Monitoring
IoT sensors at key pipelines. Citizens can check water supply status live. No more surprise shortages.
CCTV Safety Network
Strategic CCTV placement at key intersections. Footage audited monthly. Incidents resolved faster.
48-Hour Resolution
Every citizen complaint gets a guaranteed 48-hour SLA with named officer accountability and escalation.
Waste Management
GPS-tracked garbage vehicles, segregation enforcement, and citizen feedback for missed pickups.
Green Ward Initiative
Tree plantation drives, park revival, and green cover tracking — making Jubilee Hills greener every year.
The Process

How This System Works

Simple, transparent, and trackable — from complaint to resolution in five clear steps.

1
📱
Raise Complaint
Via WhatsApp, app, or missed call. Takes 30 seconds. No office visit needed.
2
🗄️
System Logs Issue
Instantly categorized and recorded. You receive a unique ticket ID via SMS.
3
👤
Assigned to Department
Right department, right officer, named accountability — no passing the buck.
4
⏱️
Time-Bound Resolution
Mandatory 48-hour SLA. Escalation triggers automatically if deadline is missed.
5
📊
Public Tracking
Every complaint, every resolution — visible on the public ward dashboard. No hiding.
